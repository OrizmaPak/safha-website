## Sales Page

The Sales Page is designed to facilitate the creation and management of sales transactions. Below is a detailed explanation of each section and its functionalities:

## Header
- **SALE**: Indicates the current page.
- **Export Button**: Allows users to export sales data.

## Customer Information
- **Customer Name**: Dropdown menu to select the customer's name.
- **Salesperson**: Dropdown menu to select the salesperson handling the transaction.
- **Order Date**: Calendar icon to select the date of the order.
- **Due Date**: Calendar icon to select the due date for the order.

## Shipping Information
- **Ship To Address**: Section to add or select the shipping address. Use the 'Add New' button to enter a new address.

## Order Summary
This section provides a detailed summary of the products included in the sales order:
- **Product ID**: Unique identifier for each product.
- **Description**: Brief description of the product.
- **Quantity**: Number of units ordered.
- **Unit Price**: Price per unit of the product.
- **Discount %**: Percentage discount applied to the product.
- **Total**: Total cost for each product line after applying the discount.

## Financial Summary
At the bottom of the page, you will find a summary of the financial details:
- **Subtotal**: Total cost of all products before tax and shipping.
- **Tax Rate**: Option to add tax to the subtotal.
- **Shipping Cost**: Option to add shipping cost.
- **Grand Total**: Final total amount including tax and shipping.

## Payment Information
- **Account Paid?**: Checkbox to indicate if the account has been paid.

## Actions
- **Save Draft**: Button to save the sales order as a draft.
- **Confirm Sale**: Button to finalize and confirm the sale.

This page is essential for managing sales transactions efficiently, ensuring all necessary details are captured and processed correctly.
